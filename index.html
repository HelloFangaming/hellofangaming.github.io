Hello Fangaming's largest and most ambitious game!

Isekai Mario includes a scenario system, where each scenario has its own story, star counter, and hub world. Each scenario is its own game! You can play them in any order, and use any playable character in any scenario! Isekai Mario will continue to be updated with new scenarios and playable characters! Going forward, all of Hello Fangaming's future games will be released as scenarios instead of as standalone games! What will the future hold in store for Isekai Mario?

Hello Fangaming

Take fangames to the next dimension!

Hello Fangaming is a long-time fangame developer who has been developing fangames for years, and is currently focused on 3D games! Hello Fangaming's current main project is Isekai Mario, a massive 3D Mario fangame containing over a hundred power stars, and hundreds of playable characters! The "Take fangames to the next dimension!" slogan represents not only the change from 2D to 3D, but also the crossing over of multiple worlds!
