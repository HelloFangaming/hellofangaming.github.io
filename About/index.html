Games for weebs by weebs!

Hello Fangaming (ヘロー・ファンゲーミング) is a weeaboo game developer focused on making anime games, and is a long-time developer that has been making fangames since 2006. Hello Fangaming's main project is Isekai Emblem, a large and ambitious Fire Emblem anime crossover tactical RPG featuring characters from a wide variety of isekai anime series!

History

Hello Fangaming is a long-time developer that has been making fangames since 2006, and originally started off with the creation of Mario fangames using GameMaker. More detailed information on these old games can be found in the Hello Fangaming Collection, which is not only a collection of old remastered levels, but a game that serves as an autobiography on past Hello Fangaming games. Hello Fangaming eventually made the transition from GameMaker to Unity, and from Mario fangames to anime games, which is the current focus. The period of time Hello Fangaming released non-anime games (2006-2019) is designated as Hello Fangaming's kurorekishi (Dark history, An embarrassing past), and Hello Fangaming's best games are the anime games that come after this dark period. Hello Fangaming's kurorekishi can be found in the Hello Fangaming Time Capsule. Nowadays, Hello Fangaming mainly works on Isekai Emblem, a Fire Emblem isekai anime crossover game that will continue to be updated with new content regularly!

After 18 years of making Mario fangames, Hello Fangaming made the decision to permanently quit Mario fangames in order to focus on anime games. Isekai Mario is Hello Fangaming's final Mario game as well as Hello Fangaming's largest and most ambitious Mario game. While Yoshi Tanks Kawaii uses some Mario style graphics, it does not count as a Mario fangame due to the game having nothing to do with Mario. In addition to this, Hello Fangaming currently has no plans to make any new platformers in general.

"While Isekai Mario has come to an end, the ultimate anime crossover will live on in Isekai Emblem, a Fire Emblem anime crossover game!"

Frequently Asked Questions

Will you ever update any of your old Mario games to fix bugs or add new features?

No, and please don't ask for updates! Hello Fangaming has quit Mario fangames and will no longer be updating any of them. All of these old Mario projects are considered complete!

Can I play your games on Android?

Yes, all of Hello Fangaming's anime games are available on both Windows and Android! However, the old Mario fangames will not be ported!

Can I record videos or stream myself playing your games?

Yes. You don't need to ask for permission, just do it! Feel free to advertise your videos or streams in the comments section for whatever game you are playing!

I want to make my own fangame! Where can I get the graphics, sounds, and other resources I need?

Check out The Spriters Resource! They have not only sprites, but also sounds, 3D models, and textures. Also make sure to check out Hello Mario Framework if you want an easy way to make your own 3D Mario game!

I finished making a game! Where can I go to upload and share it with others?

You can upload your games to itch.io. Also, if you use Hello Mario Framework to make a game, you are allowed to advertise your game in the framework's comments section!

Can I make a commercial game using Hello Mario Framework?

You can! However, the Super Mario models, textures, animations, and audio clips included with the framework are for free non-profit fangame projects only. If you intend to use Hello Mario Framework in a commercial project, you must replace all of these assets with your own!

I used your framework or code in my game. Can I give credit to Hello?

Please don't! Use the name Hello Fangaming in your credits. Thank you!

Are you still active? What fangame projects are you currently working on?

Yes, Hello Fangaming is still actively working on Isekai Emblem! For the latest news, you can follow Hello Fangaming on Bluesky, and subscribe to Hello Fangaming on YouTube!

Logo

The Hello Fangaming Logo was created in 2023 and is a simplified and more modern version of an older logo created in 2011. This logo is included in all anime games released by Hello Fangaming. The icon represents Mario, which is where Hello Fangaming began, and the Katakana text represents the future of Hello Fangaming, which is anime.

2011 Logo

The old Hello Fangaming Logo was created in 2011 by Hello Fangaming. This logo is part of Hello Fangaming's kurorekishi, was included in the older Mario games released between 2011 and 2023, and was updated with .svg (Scalable Vector Graphics) in 2022. If you look closely, you can kind of see Mario in the icon.
