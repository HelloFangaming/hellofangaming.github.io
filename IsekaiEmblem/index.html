Tactics in Another World

Fire Emblem and anime come together as one in Isekai Emblem - Tactics in Another World! The story begins when you are hit by a truck, and reincarnate into a fantasy world as a divine dragon! It is now up to you to command an army made up of reincarnated anime characters from a wide variety of isekai franchises in this large and ambitious tactical RPG crossover!

About

You have been reincarnated as a divine dragon in another world!

Isekai Emblem features characters from a wide variety of different isekai anime franchises including Sword Art Online, Re:ZERO, KonoSuba, Overlord, That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime, Shield Hero, Mushoku Tensei, and more! The game includes a quest system, where in each quest, you will experience an array of story cutscenes and tactics RPG battles. After clearing the prologue quest, the game will open up, and you will be able to play quests in any order!

Unfortunately, the tactical RPG part of the game was not ready in time for SAGE 2025, so instead, this demo only includes minigames! The gallery is also included, which you can use to learn about various isekai anime and customize the music used in the game. How much of a weeb are you?
