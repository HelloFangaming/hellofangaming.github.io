Yoshi Tanks Kawaii

In Yoshi Tanks Kawaii, play as up to six different Yoshi Tank Girls and use a variety of different powerups as you fire away at your opponents in this multiplayer turn-based artillery game, whether they be your friends, or AI controlled!

About

Anime Artillery Game!

Yoshi Tanks Kawaii is the successor to Yoshi Tanks, a minigame released by Hello Fangaming back in 2013. But this time, more kawaii than ever before! The tanks have been reborn as Yoshi Tank Girls! Experience the all new story mode, play with up to six tanks, and fight in larger maps in this enhanced artillery game! The game is now available on Android for the very first time and supports different aspect ratios, which can make the maps even wider than ever before! Pass off your mobile phone or tablet between your friends with this turn-based artillery game!

News

Latest version: v1.0.2

Yoshi Tanks Kawaii v1.0.2 is now available for download

October 17, 2025

Yoshi Tanks Kawaii returns with various fixes and improvements, as well as a new option to spawn in more powerups!

  • Updated powerup options in Free Play: Off, Low (Previously: On), Medium (Previously: Crazy, the new default), and High (New!).
  • Warp whistles will no longer warp you over bottomless pits. (Unless you have a balloon)
  • Hold to shoot is slightly faster.
  • Bug fixes.

Yoshi Tanks Kawaii v1.0.1 is now available for download

June 20, 2025

Due to the P-Balloon and Shield combination being overpowered, these powerups have been updated to no longer stack. In addition, the UI sliders now have larger hitboxes, making them easier to manipulate on smaller phones.

  • P-Balloon and Shields no longer stack.
  • Larger hitboxes on UI sliders.
  • Bug fixes.

Yoshi Tanks Kawaii v1.0 is now available for download

May 31, 2025

The successor to 2013's Yoshi Tanks is finally here! This initial release is available on both Windows and Android!
