Super Mario Prism

Synopsis: The Sprixie princess has been trapped in one of the painting worlds! It is now up to Mario to rescue her by collecting the power stars to restore color to the paintings in this 3D platformer!

Release Date: March 15, 2019

Genre: 3D

Trivia

  • Super Mario Prism is Hello Fangaming's first 3D Mario game, and first fangame made in Unity!
  • The game contains four painting worlds, with plenty of power stars to collect.
  • Toadette's Sweets Gallery is the successor to this game, and reuses the same engine.
  • Shortly after the game's release, the version 1.0.1 update of Super Mario Prism was released. This update tweaked the physics, sped up some animations, fixed some bugs, disabled Unity's axis smoothing on keyboard controls, and disabled more special effects on low quality settings.
  • After another week passed, the version 1.0.2 update of Super Mario Prism was released. This update tweaked the physics again, fixed more bugs, and added some normal maps that were missing.
  • After another month passed, the version 1.0.3 update of Super Mario Prism was released. This update tweaked the physics again, fixed more bugs, and added mouse camera controls.
