Hello Winter Mario Games 2009

Synopsis: This winter game includes winter remakes of Super Luigi Paradise and Mario's Run. In addition to this, extra new levels have been added.

Release Date: December 1, 2009

Genre: Platform/Minigame

Download on Indie DB

Trivia

  • Super Luigi Paradise was fully recreated within the Hello Mario Engine, and you can play as both Mario and Luigi in both of these games for the first time!
  • Hello Winter Mario Games 2009 was originally going to become a part of a series of yearly winter remakes of old games. However, that ended up never happening, so we are now left with a single game with a mess of a name.
  • Icey Cavern and Glowing Grotto were later recreated in Hello Fangaming Collection as Eclipse Cavern and Glittering Grotto.
