Super Crown Land

Synopsis: Why kidnap the princess when you can become the princess? With the power of the Super Crown, anyone can become one, including Bowser! However, not everyone in the Koopa Kingdom thinks this is a good idea. It is now up to Bowsette to put an end to this rebellion, and reunite the Koopa Kingdom under the rule of their new princess!

Release Date: May 15, 2020

Genre: 3D

Trivia

  • Super Crown Land is currently Hello Fangaming's definitive 3D Mario game.
  • Super Crown Land is the successor to Toadette's Sweets Gallery, with tons of new features and improvements to its 3D engine.
  • This game contains ten playable characters, more than any other Hello Fangaming game. These characters include Bowsette, Mario, Luigi, Sonic, Infinite, Human-Size Bowsette, Naruto, Neptune, Rem, and Kanna. This beats the previous record of eight playable characters set by Shroom Editor.
  • This game is Hello Fangaming's largest crossover game, with characters from many different franchises including Super Mario, Sonic the Hedgehog, Naruto, Hyperdimension Neptunia, Re:ZERO, and Miss Kobayashi's Dragon Maid.
  • The game contains six painting worlds, each featuring a different Super Crown character.
  • Hidden throughout Super Crown Land are secret collectable cards featuring artwork for a whopping 24 different Super Crown transformations. Can you find and collect them all?
  • On September 2020, the SAGE 2020 update was released. You can still check out its SAGE booth right here. This update added a new playable character, Infinite from Sonic Forces, alongside various tweaks and bug fixes.
