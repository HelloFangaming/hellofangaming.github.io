Hello Fangaming
This is considered part of Hello Fangaming's kurorekishi (Dark history, An embarrassing past). Try not to take it too seriously.

Welcome to the Hello Fangaming Wiki! Here, you will find information on Hello Fangaming's kurorekishi, a dark period of time spanning about a decade and a half when Hello Fangaming used to make Mario fangames before finally seeing the light and moving on to make anime games. Because Hello Fangaming was a prominent member of the Mario fangames community in the past, the information here has been preserved.


This wiki only covers Hello Fangaming's kurorekishi, so new projects will not be listed here.

  • Visit the About page to learn more about Hello Fangaming!
