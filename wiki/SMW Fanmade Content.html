Super Mario War Fanmade Content

The Super Mario War Fanmade Content Website was created in 2006 to host fanmade content for the game Super Mario War, and was shut down in 2019. An archive of the site is available on the Wayback Machine, and you can download the fanmade content below. Please note that the content on this page is old, and is available for archival purposes.

Maps

  • SMW Fan Maps and Worlds

    Contains 8 worlds and 234 maps.

  • Custom Tileset Maps

    Contains 3 tilesets, 43 backgrounds, and 228 maps ported over from the Super Mario War 2007, Custom Mario War, and 8-Bit Mario War mods so that they can be used in Super Mario War 1.8. Also contains converters you can use to convert any maps you made for Custom Mario War and Super Mario War 2007 into 1.8 custom tileset maps.

  • SMW Maps and Worlds

    Includes maps by various authors. Contains 4 worlds and 479 maps.
