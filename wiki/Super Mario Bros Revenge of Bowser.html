Super Mario Bros: Revenge of Bowser

Synopsis: Oh no! Princess Peach has been trapped by Bowser! Go through pipes in Peach's castle which take you all around the Mushroom Kingdom in this classic platformer, and collect the stars by finishing a level, finding 5 Ace coins, and finding the hidden shine. Can you collect all 120 stars in 40 levels, and defeat Bowser?

Release Date: January 19, 2008

Genre: Platform

Trivia

  • Hello Fangaming's first game to use the third Hello Mario Engine version, and first to feature a hub area.
  • You need to clear each level three times to collect all the stars. Once, just to finish the level, again to collect all five ace coins, and finally again to collect the level's hidden shine.
  • You have the option to pay three lives to enter a mushroom house and get a free powerup.
  • The game includes 10 worlds with 4 levels each.
  • This game is longer than all the games Hello Fangaming released previously with a total of 40 levels. This record was not surpassed until the release of Super Mario Bros: Restless Reality.
  • Two of this game's levels were recreated in the Hello Fangaming Collection.
