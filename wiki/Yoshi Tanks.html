Yoshi Tanks

Synopsis: In Yoshi Tanks, the aim of the game is to take out all of your opponent tanks, whether they are your friends, or AI controlled.

Release Date: September 5, 2013

Genre: Minigame

Download on Indie DB

Trivia

  • This game is based on an old DOS classic, but this time, with a Mario twist!
  • Yoshi Tanks was actually scrapped back in 2012. After rediscovering the source file a year later, Hello Fangaming decided to port it to GameMaker: Studio, finish it up, and release it.
  • This is Hello Fangaming's first widescreen game, and Hello Fangaming's only game to feature the 512x288 resolution.
  • Yoshi Tanks is, unfortunately, Hello Fangaming's least popular game, and sports fewer downloads and views than any other Hello Fangaming game.
