Time Capsule Game Recommendations

Did you download the Hello Fangaming Time Capsule + Launcher? Are you overwhelmed by the number of games included? Are you not sure what game to play first? If so, this page is for you! Select any of the following choices below to get a game recommended to you!

What are you looking for?

  • I just want to play some levels!
  • I want a challenge!
  • I want to solve puzzles!
  • I want guns and blood!
  • I want to explore!
  • I want something different!

I just want to play some levels!

Hello Fangaming Collection

If you're not sure where to even start, then the Hello Fangaming Collection would be a good choice. It includes remastered versions of levels from a variety of different games found in the time capsule.

Super Mario Bros: Restless Reality

Out of all the fangames in the time capsule, Super Mario Bros: Restless Reality has the largest level count. A good choice if you want to play lots and lots of levels.

Super Mario Bros: Kingdom Troubles

Out of the time capsule's older fangames, Super Mario Bros: Kingdom Troubles fits in this category, with a decent amount of levels packed within.

I want a challenge!

Super Mario Dynamo

Looking to be challenged? If so, check out Super Mario Dynamo. The game includes many challenging levels that require the use of Mario's wall jump abilities.

Super Mario Bros: Fierce Days

Another good choice would be Super Mario Bros: Fierce Days, the time capsule's hardest fangame.

Super Mario Bros: Dark Days

Out of the time capsule's older fangames, Super Mario Bros: Dark Days is the most difficult, although it leans on the unfair side...

I want to solve puzzles!

Super Mario Eclipse

Looking for a game with lots of puzzles? Check out Super Mario Eclipse! This game includes a wide variety of color based puzzles.

Super Mario Bros: Seeking Sunshine

Another good choice for puzzles is Super Mario Bros: Seeking Sunshine. This game utilises on/off blocks in its puzzles.

Super Mario Bros: Dual Dash

Out of the time capsule's older fangames, Super Mario Bros: Dual Dash fits in this category, with puzzles that require using both Mario and Luigi to solve.

I want guns and blood!

Super Mario Bros: Ztar Turmoil

Do you want to see guns and blood in your Mario games? Look no further than Super Mario Bros: Ztar Turmoil, the time capsule's definitive gun game.

Super Mario Bros: Bloody Battles

Another good choice for gun games is Super Mario Bros: Bloody Battles. This game makes less changes to the base Mario gameplay than the time capsule's other gun games.

Super Mario Bros: Crimson Hours

Out of the time capsule's older fangames, Super Mario Bros: Crimson Hours fits in this category, with its own take on the guns and blood genre.

I want to explore!

Super Mario Bros: Mythical Mushrooms

Would you rather explore a large world instead of playing level after level? If so check out Super Mario Bros: Mythical Mushrooms, a metroidvania style Mario game.

Super Mario Bros: Shine Expedition

Another good choice for exploration is Super Mario Bros: Shine Expedition. This game includes multiple large levels that can be explored.

Super Luigi and the Golden Shrooms

Out of the time capsule's older fangames, Super Luigi and the Golden Shrooms is a good choice for exploring, although it is smaller than the other games listed above.

I want something different!

Yoshi Tanks

Looking for something different? Check out Yoshi Tanks, the newest minigame included in the time capsule.

Present Panic

Another good choice for minigames is Present Panic, a puzzle game about pushing presents into pipes.

Save the Goombas

Out of the time capsule's older minigames, Save the Goombas fits in this category, with its mouse based puzzles.
