The Shroom Project

Synopsis: The zombie apocalypse is nigh! The world is in utter chaos, and death is commonplace. A malicious group is responsible for all of this destruction, and is busy at work on a dark scheme known as The Shroom Project. Play as one of two characters, join a rag tag team known as The Resistance, and fight back against a mysterious, yet, familiar face!

Release Date: December 22, 2017

Genre: Shooter

Trivia

  • The Shroom Project features two playable custom Toad characters, and a colorful cast of unique Toad NPCs.
  • This is Hello Fangaming's first game to feature a female playable character.
  • The gun mechanics from Super Mario Bros: Ztar Turmoil make a return with many improvements and balance changes.
