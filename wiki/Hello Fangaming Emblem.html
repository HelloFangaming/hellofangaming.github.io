Hello Fangaming Emblem

The Hello Fangaming Emblem was created on 2011 by Hello Fangaming, and updated with .svg (Scalable Vector Graphics) versions on 2022. This emblem was included in all games released by Hello Fangaming afterwards. If you look closely, you can kind of see Mario in the emblem.
