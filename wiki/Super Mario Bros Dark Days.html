Super Mario Bros: Dark Days

Synopsis: Mario with guns and blood!

Release Date: March 18, 2007

Genre: Shooter

Download on Indie DB

Note: This game is included in the Hello Fangaming Time Capsule.

Trivia

  • Just like Mario's Egg, this game uses Martin Piecyk's platform engine.
  • This is Hello Fangaming's first shooting game. The next one to be made is Super Mario Bros: Crimson Hours.
  • Due to the lack of health system, Mario always dies in one hit, making this one of Hello Fangaming's most difficult games.
  • This is the last game by Hello Fangaming to uses someone else's engine.
  • The abbreviation of this game is SMB:DD. This abbreviation is used again with Super Mario Bros: Dual Dash. Surprisingly, out of all of Hello Fangaming's games, this is the only case of this happening.
