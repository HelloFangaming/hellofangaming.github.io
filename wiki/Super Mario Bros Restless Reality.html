Super Mario Bros: Restless Reality

Synopsis: Many years have passed since the Mario Bros last defeated Bowser and stopped his plans for world domination. The Mushroom Kingdom enjoyed years of peace. Until one day... Bowser is up to no good again! He is invading the once peaceful kingdom, and setting up forts! What could he be planning!? Mario must travel through many different locations to find out what Bowser is up to and stop his evil plans!

Release Date: August 8, 2010

Genre: Platform

Trivia

  • Currently, this game holds the record for largest level count in any of Hello Fangaming's 2D games with a whopping fifty levels!
  • This game features the health circle, and the trend of including this feature continues in Hello Fangaming's future games.
  • This is Hello Fangaming's last standard Mario platformer to feature no special gameplay gimmicks to set it apart from other Mario platformers.
  • Pleasant Creek, Disco Drainage Quandry, Showy Skies, and Conflagrant Cavity were later recreated in Hello Fangaming Collection. All of these levels kept their original names except for Showy Skies, which was renamed to Sunset Skies.
  • A bonus file for this game was placed on the original Hello Fangaming website for people to find. Extracting the file to the same folder as the game executable would allow you to start a new game with 50,000 coins. You can still download that bonus file right here!
